A small studio with a
big love for flowers.

Ohara Flora began at a single bench in Sydney, with a flower market run before sunrise and a belief that a good bunch can change someone's whole day.

We still tie every arrangement by hand, choose stems by what is freshest that morning, and never let a bouquet leave the studio unless we would happily send it to our own mum.
